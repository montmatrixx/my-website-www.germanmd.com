Frequently Asked Questions

Is GermanMD a replacement for my doctor?

No. We provide an independent expert opinion to help you make more informed decisions. We always recommend working closely with your treating physician.

How do I share my medical documents securely?

After booking via email or WhatsApp, we provide a secure method for sharing your documents. All records are handled with strict medical confidentiality.

How long does it take to receive my report?

Most cases are reviewed within 48–72 hours. Complex cases requiring specialist input may take slightly longer — we will always inform you in advance.

Which conditions do you cover?

We cover most medical specialties including oncology, cardiology, neurology, orthopaedics, gastroenterology, endocrinology, and more. Contact us to confirm your specific case.

Is the consultation available across India?

Yes. Our service is fully online and available to patients anywhere in India — and to Indian patients living abroad.

What language will my report be in?

Your written report will be in clear, plain English. We can also arrange a follow-up call to walk you through the findings in Hindi or your preferred language.
